Our Competitions

American Computer Science League (ACSL)

ACSL is an international competition that focuses on coding and computer science skills. Competitions run from November to May, with top scoring students invited to the Finals.

Congressional App Challenge (CAC)

A national competition for designing and developing applications, open to high school students. Winners may have their apps displayed in the U.S. Capitol.

USA Computing Olympiad (USACO)

USACO is a challenging online coding competition, where participants advance through multiple levels of difficulty, aiming to qualify for the prestigious International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI).
